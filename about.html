About Fun Fishing

Our Philosophy

No matter what your age, ability or experience level, Fishing is a great way to get active outdoors and has many proven health benefits.
Whether it is just for a quick hour at your local lake, canal or river or spending a whole day with friends and family by the sea give fishing a go and join the millions of people who enjoy it each year.

Fishing Advantages

Involves a whole range of low, medium and high intensity physical activity.
Is brilliant for relaxation and aids concentration.
Helps build up your relationships and confidence which can reduce isolation.
Allows you to become more physically active at a pace that suits you, whatever your age.
Can be incorporated with other activities such as walking or cycling to a fishing venue.
Is a good way to spend time with family and enjoy an activity together.

Costs

Most of these sessions are free or very low cost and include the use of tackle, bait and a rod-licence for the day.
After that all you will need is some low-cost tackle, a rod licence and a day-ticket or club membership to access some fishing venues.
As part of this, angling clubs, fisheries and organisations run taster sessions which give beginners the opportunity to try fishing for the first time.

Equipment needed

You can enjoy fishing with the most basic equipment available.
You can ask your local club, fishery or tackle shop to help you pick what you need to get started.
A pair of sunglasses, a hat and waterproofs or a brolly can be useful if the weather changes.
When you start fishing you will need to buy a rod licence if you are 17 or over and want to fish in freshwater, but for junior anglers rod licences are free.
