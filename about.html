About Fun Fishing

Our Philosophy

No matter what your age, ability or experience level, fishing is a great way to get active outdoors and has many proven health benefits

Fishing Advantages

Involves a whole range of physical activity. Is brilliant for relaxation and aids concentration.

Costs

Most of the fishing sessions are free or very low costs and include the use of tackle, bait and a rod-licence for the day.

Equipment needed

We can offer you all the basic equipment or you can bring with you, your equipment. Also you can bring with you a pair of sunglasses, a hat and waterproofs or a brolly.
